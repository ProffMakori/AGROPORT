About Us

AgroDist company is a transportation company with specific to agricultural products across Africa.It has it headquarters in Nairobi Kenya and operates within 55 countries in the African continent.It utilizes modern technologies to maintain the conditions necessary to withstand and prolong the lifespan of agricultural products. We value the originality of our esteemed customers.

  • Our Network

    We are proud to have a strong network across Africa. We have numerous deport centers within different regions with effective communication that provode efficient supply chain.

  • Mode of Transportation

    We use various transport models depending on the distination, nature of the product and the time specified by the client for delivery. We are currently using road, air and railway transport system.
